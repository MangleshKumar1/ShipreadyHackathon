## 📝 Submission Description (Copy-Paste Ready)

```
WHAT I BUILT:
ProdSignal turns messy customer feedback into 
evidence-backed product decisions and engineering-ready 
specs in under 60 seconds.

THE PROBLEM:
PMs spend hours reading feedback, guessing at priorities, 
and writing PRDs from memory. The result: roadmaps based 
on the loudest voice, not the real signal.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Paste any feedback (reviews, Slack, tickets, interviews)
2. AI clusters themes with opportunity scores + real quotes
3. AI makes explicit BUILD/DELAY/IGNORE decisions with proof
4. One click generates an evidence-backed PRD where every 
   user story links to actual customer quotes

THE DIFFERENTIATOR:
Not summaries. Not sentiment. Actual decisions with proof.
Every recommendation is traceable to real customer words.

BUILT WITH:
Next.js + Tailwind + shadcn/ui + Claude API + 
Supabase + Vercel + Novus.ai

WHO IT'S FOR:
PMs, founders, and product teams who are drowning in 
feedback but starving for decisions.
```
