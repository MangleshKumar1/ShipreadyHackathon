Tools and Technologies
Use whatever you want. Genuinely.
This hackathon is deliberately tool-agnostic because we think the most interesting story of the year is how many different ways product people now get things shipped. Some directions to consider:

* AI app builders: Bolt.new, Lovable, Replit Agent, v0, Figma Make, Claude Artifacts
* AI coding assistants: Cursor, Windsurf, Claude Code, GitHub Copilot
* Traditional stacks: Next.js, SvelteKit, Rails, Django, FastAPI — anything you're already fast in
* Mobile: Expo, React Native, Flutter, native iOS/Android
* No-code / low-code: Softr, Glide, Bubble, Retool, Airtable
* Backends & infra: Supabase, Firebase, Neon, Vercel, Netlify, Cloudflare
* AI APIs: Anthropic, OpenAI, Google AI Studio, ElevenLabs, Replicate
The one required piece of tech in your stack: [Novus.ai](https://novus.ai/), installed on your deployed project before submission. Novus gives you — and the judges — visibility into how people are actually using what you built. If you've never instrumented a product before, this is a good time to start.
Additional Resource links
